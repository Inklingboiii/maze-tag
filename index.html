How To Play

Use WASD or the arrow keys on pc and the arrow buttons on mobile to move and collect the golden coins to gain points while you escape from the red enemy, but be careful! If the red enemy touches a coin it turns into an impassable wall.
Note: if you get softlocked or any other problems then restart the game by pressing exit and start game or reload the page.

Settings

In the settings menu which you can open and close by pressing on the settings icon on the top right you can change the color scheme, the scale of the board and more. Your changes get saved automatically.
