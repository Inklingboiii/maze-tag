How To Play

Use WASD to move and collect the golden coins to gain points while you escape from the red enemy, but be careful! If the red enemy touches a coin it turns into an inpassable wall.

Settings

In the settings menu you can change the color scheme, the scale of the board and more. Your changes get saved automatically.
